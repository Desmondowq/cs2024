FAQ

Q: What is the sound quality like for AirBud?

AirBud delivers a high quality sound that is perfect for your favorite music and features premium quad high-definition neodymium titanium drivers that deliver both clear treble and incredible bass.

Q: What kind of control will I have over the glowing?

With the AirBud app, you can take DJing to the next level with glowing music visualisations illuminating from the wire. Colors are an unique expression of individuality and opens up limitless personalisation that pulses to the sound of your favourite tunes.

Q: How long will AirBud light up on a single charge?

We incorporated the charging mechanism as a glowing magnetic wire that charges straight from any audio jack. The wire is capable of illuminating the full spectrum of colours by using a low powered OLED embedded at the end of the audio jack. On a full charge you will get up to an incredible 14 hours of listening pleasure.
